## Setting Up Health Monitoring

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Health Scores">
    Create a scoring system based on:
    - Login frequency (40%)
    - Feature usage (30%)
    - Support tickets (20%)
    - Engagement (10%)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Intent Signals">
    Set up alerts for critical pages:
    - `/pricing` â†’ Expansion opportunity
    - `/cancel` â†’ Churn risk
    - `/docs/api` â†’ Technical investment
    - `/integrations` â†’ Deeper adoption
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Segments">
    Build customer segments:
    - By health score
    - By contract value
    - By renewal date
    - By product tier
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up Alerts">
    Configure Slack alerts for:
    - Health score drops >20%
    - Cancellation page visits
    - New user additions
    - Feature limit approaches
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Proactive Playbooks

### When Health Score Drops

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Immediate Actions">
    1. **Review Recent Activity**
       - Check last 30 days of usage
       - Look for specific friction points
       - Identify which features declined
    
    2. **Reach Out Within 24 Hours**
       ```
       Subject: Quick check-in on your [Product] experience
       
       Hi [Name],
       
       I noticed your team's usage has shifted recently, and I wanted to 
       check in to see if everything is going smoothly.
       
       Are there any challenges or questions I can help address?
       
       Happy to jump on a quick call if helpful.
       ```
    
    3. **Schedule Business Review**
       - Review original goals
       - Assess current state
       - Identify gaps
       - Create action plan
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Long-term Strategy">
    1. **Deepen Relationships**
       - Identify new champions
       - Executive alignment
       - Success story development
    
    2. **Drive Adoption**
       - Feature training
       - Best practices sharing
       - Use case expansion
    
    3. **Demonstrate Value**
       - ROI analysis
       - Benchmark reports
       - Success metrics
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Best Practices

### 1. Response Time Matters

| Signal Type | Response Time | Method |
|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Cancellation research | < 2 hours | Phone call |
| Health drop >30% | < 24 hours | Email + call |
| Expansion signal | < 48 hours | Email |
| General decline | < 1 week | Check-in |

### 2. Personalization is Key

Use Koala data to personalize outreach:
- Reference specific features they use
- Mention pages they've visited
- Address their actual use case
- Provide relevant resources

### 3. Track Intervention Success

Measure the impact of your actions:
- Health score recovery rate
- Churn prevention success
- Expansion conversion rate
- Time to resolution

## Common Patterns to Watch

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The Silent Churn">
    **Pattern**: Gradual usage decline with no support contact
    
    **Action**: Proactive check-in focusing on value realization
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="The Researcher">
    **Pattern**: Heavy documentation and competitor research
    
    **Action**: Technical consultation and feature roadmap discussion
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="The Limit Hitter">
    **Pattern**: Consistently hitting plan limits
    
    **Action**: Upgrade conversation with ROI justification
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="The New Stakeholder">
    **Pattern**: New domain/users with different behavior
    
    **Action**: Re-onboarding and relationship building
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
